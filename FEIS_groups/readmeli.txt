To get 'reviews.csv' go to: https://www.feis-crs.org/feis/
Then under 'Find Species Reviews' click [Find All].
Then click [Export Table] to download 'reviews.csv'.
*reviews.csv is not going to be used.

How to get: 
plants.csv
animals.csv
lichens.csv

Go to: https://www.feis-crs.org/feis/
Then under 'Find Species Reviews' click [Advanced Search].
Then click on ‘Life form’ checkbox
Then now you can choose from: Plants, Animals, Lichens.
Click [Go], then [Export Table] to download respective CSV file.

*Fungi doesn’t have any species as of this writing (July 3, 2018).
